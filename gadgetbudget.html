My gadget budget

This page keeps track of my most expensive purchases (typically gadgets) and tracks their total usage, and thus their normalized cost. The basic idea was explained in this post. Shoes are omitted, as their cost and total lifetime usage is well known, not as interesting, and usually comes down to 6 ¢/km or 10 ¢/mi.

Quick note: often only a subset of the total usage is considered here, as other type of activities, or the same type of activities by others are neglected.
